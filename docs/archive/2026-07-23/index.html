2AL 모범답변 + 전략 해설

🔒 스스로 답한 후 열어보세요 — 탭해서 열기

Well, to answer your question, I currently live in a medium-sized apartment with my family. Overall, it has a modern open-concept layout, which makes the entire place feel surprisingly spacious and airy.

If I had to pick my absolute favorite spot, it would definitely be my living room. You know, the highlight of this room is the floor-to-ceiling windows that let in ample natural light all day long. Honestly, walking into the room bathed in soft sunlight every morning instantly brightens up my mood. Right in the middle, I placed a soft velvet sofa that serves as a focal point, which really helps to create a warm ambiance.

Also, because I frequently work from home, I set up a dedicated workstation in one corner. By carefully picking low-profile furniture, I was able to maximize space efficiency without making the room feel cramped.

Looking back, when I first moved in, the apartment was completely bare. But taking the time to turn it into a space that feels cozy and inviting has been immensely satisfying. All in all, my home isn't just a physical space; it's my personal sanctuary where I can fully reset.

🧭 답변 뼈대
서론: 거주 형태 및 아파트 전체 구조(open-concept layout) 소개 → MP: 가장 좋아하는 공간인 거실 소개 → 디테일 1: 통유리창(floor-to-ceiling windows), 채광, 분위기를 잡는 가구배치 → 디테일 2: 재택근무 공간 조성을 통한 공간 활용(maximize space efficiency) 극대화 → 감정/생각: 빈 집을 꾸미며 느낀 큰 만족감 → 마무리: 단순한 집을 넘어 정서적 안식처라는 최종 의미 부여

이 답변이 AL인 이유 3가지

✨ 감각적이고 구체적인 공간 묘사 어휘: 'floor-to-ceiling windows', 'bathed in soft sunlight', 'focal point' 등 시각적 이미지를 선명하게 전달하는 고급 어휘 표현을 자유롭게 구사했습니다.
✨ 자연스러운 구어체 디스코스 마커 사용: 'You know', 'Honestly', 'Looking back', 'All in all'과 같은 연결어를 적재적소에 배치하여 답변의 흐름을 대화하듯 부드럽게 이끌었습니다.
✨ 시제 및 상황 대비(Past vs. Present): 과거 처음 이사 왔을 때의 상태(completely bare)와 현재 직접 정성스레 꾸민 상태(feels cozy and inviting)를 대조하여 세련된 서사 구조를 완성했습니다.
